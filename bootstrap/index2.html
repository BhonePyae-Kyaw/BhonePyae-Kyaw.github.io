Sapa

Sapa is a picturesque town located in the northern mountains of Vietnam, known for its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Surrounded by terraced rice fields and dramatic peaks, Sapa offers breathtaking views and a unique opportunity to explore the traditional lifestyles of the local ethnic minority groups, such as the Hmong and Dao.

Kyaik Htee Yoe

Kyaik Htee Yoe, also known as the Golden Rock, is a prominent Buddhist pilgrimage site in Myanmar. Located in Mon State, this unique rock formation is famous for its gold-leaf-covered boulder that precariously balances on the edge of a cliff.
